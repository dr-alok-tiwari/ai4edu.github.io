About the Curator

Dr. Alok Tiwari

I am an Assistant Professor, researcher, and applied AI educator working at the intersection of medical imaging AI, healthcare analytics, and data-driven decision-making. With a PhD in Biomedical Engineering from IIT (BHU) and academic experience across management, engineering, and executive education, I bring a blend of research depth, teaching excellence, and real-world AI application.

My work focuses on machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and weakly supervised methods, especially for healthcare applications such as chest X-ray classification, cardiac MRI segmentation, and clinical AI systems. Alongside research, I actively design courses, mentor students, and conduct faculty and industry development programmes in AI, analytics, and emerging technologies.

I am committed to building responsible, explainable, and impactful AI solutions that connect academic research with practical implementation in healthcare, management, and digital innovation.
